MY HOLIDAY

My European Love

In Europe with my Family

My family and I went to Europe for a holiday before Chirstmas, I was really excited because I have always wanted to go to Europe. The old Architecture, was one thing i wanted to see but seeing it in person made me love Europe even more It was such an interesting thing to see, all the wonders so well preserved.

The only problem

The only problem was how cold it was, my parents and I were shivering all the time, only when we were in a store were we free from the daunting coldness. Even though it was so cold, there was no snow, i was really sad to not see any because i have never seen snow before but always wanted to see it. I still want to thank my parents for taking me on this trip i hope i can pay them back one day.
